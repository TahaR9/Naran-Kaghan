Naran Valley

Naran is a town and popular tourist destination in upper Kaghan Valley in Mansehra District of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan. It is located 119 kilometers from Mansehra city at the altitude of 2,409 meters. It is located about 65 kilometers away from Babusar Top.

When to visit?

The best time to visit Naran is in the summer months due to less probability of snow fall and blocked roads but the city is accessible in the summer months as well. During summer months the temperature can go up to 20 degree centigrade and in winters drops well beyond the freezing point due to heavy snowfall.

Distance from Islamabad?

The distance between Islamabad and Kaghan is approximately 261 km (162 miles) which is roughly just over 5 hours travel time.

What to wear?

When you are visiting Naran Kaghan in summers, try to get warm clothes, extra pair of jeans and shocks. Because Naran weather is unpredictable at times. In winters, you really need some overcoats, long warm boost, thick shocks, and hats to cover your ears.
